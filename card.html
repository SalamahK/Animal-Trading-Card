Domestic Cat

According to Veterinary Hub, cats usually spend 70% of their lies sleeping, which works out to be around 13-16 hours a day.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length (without tail): 46cm
  • Average Lifespan: 2-16 years
  • Habitat: House

A domestic cat averages about 46cm(18in) in head to body length, 23-35cm in height with about 30cm lomg tails. Males are typically larger than females. Adult domestic cats typically weigh between 4 and 5kg.They have excellent night vision and can see at only one-soxth the light level required for human vision.They have an acute sense of smell.
